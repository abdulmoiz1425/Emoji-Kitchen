🧑‍🍳 What it is

What Is an Emoji Kitchen?

A Google feature that mixes compatible emojis into custom stickers — available right in your browser.

Emoji Kitchen is a Google feature that mixes compatible emojis into custom stickers. Instead of sending two separate symbols, users can share one picture that blends both ideas into a single visual reaction.

Google first introduced the feature through Gboard on Android. It is also available through Google Search, giving more users a way to make combinations from a phone, tablet, or computer browser.

The results are sticker-style pictures, not new standard emojis. They may send as images inside messages, which helps the design stay clear across many apps and devices.
